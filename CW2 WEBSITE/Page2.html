    There are many techniques and methods to employ when creating a piece of artwork, but you don't need the world's best equipment to make anything. Even if you're very limited to what you can do, sometimes all you need is paper, a pencil and a pen.

    Techniques

    But, if you want to use anything other than a pen or pencil, some of the easiest methods when creating artwork include painting with either acrylic or watercolour - not only is acrylic inexpensive, but it drys very quickly. Its versatility also makes for a beautiful creation. Alike with watercolour, it is also inexpensive and easy to use. Although watercolour can be unforgiving at times due to its fluid nature, it can be a fun and expressive method to explore.
